🌟 Chapter 3: Headings & Paragraphs

Learn how to structure text using headings and paragraphs — very important in HTML!

What are Headings?

Headings define titles and subtitles on a webpage. They range from <h1> (most important) to <h6> (least important).

Example of headings:

<h1>This is H1 - Main Title</h1>
<h2>This is H2 - Sub Title</h2>
<h3>This is H3 - Section Title</h3>

What are Paragraphs?

Paragraphs represent blocks of text. They are written inside <p> tags.

Example of paragraph:

<p>This is a paragraph of text. It can hold multiple sentences.</p>
